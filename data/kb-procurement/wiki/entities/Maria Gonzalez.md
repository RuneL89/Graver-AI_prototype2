# Maria Gonzalez

## Summary

Signatory Director on contract PC-2025-00442 (Port Infrastructure Upgrade, USD 4,200,000) for Oceanic Logistics Ltd.

## Details

Maria Gonzalez is recorded in the 2025 procurement contracts dataset as the signatory director for Oceanic Logistics Ltd on contract PC-2025-00442 (source: procurement_contracts_2025.pdf). The contract, a Port Infrastructure Upgrade, was awarded for USD 4,200,000 and signed on 2025-08-15.

At USD 4,200,000, this is the largest contract in the 2025 dataset and significantly exceeds the [[public procurement threshold]] (source: procurement_contracts_2025.pdf). Contracts of this magnitude typically require open competitive bidding, independent evaluation panels, and public disclosure of award rationale. Journalists should investigate whether these procedures were followed.

Gonzalez does not appear on any sanctions lists, but her co-director Jens Hansen was later sanctioned for procurement fraud (source: sanctions_list_march_2026.pdf). This does not imply wrongdoing by Gonzalez, but it raises questions about due diligence conducted by the awarding authority.
