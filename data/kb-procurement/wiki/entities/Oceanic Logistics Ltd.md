# Oceanic Logistics Ltd

## Summary

Government vendor awarded contract PC-2025-00442 (Port Infrastructure Upgrade, USD 4,200,000).

## Details

Oceanic Logistics Ltd appears in the 2025 procurement contracts dataset as the vendor on contract PC-2025-00442 (source: procurement_contracts_2025.pdf). The contract, a Port Infrastructure Upgrade, was the largest award in the dataset at USD 4,200,000, signed by Maria Gonzalez on 2025-08-15.

The company is registered at 45 Harbor Road, Port City, with directors Maria Gonzalez and Jens Hansen (source: business_registry_q1_2026.pdf). While Gonzalez is not sanctioned, Hansen was later sanctioned for procurement fraud and money laundering (source: sanctions_list_march_2026.pdf). The company also operates in a market segment related to NorthStar Procurement Inc (via the shared director Hansen), creating a potential [[conflict of interest]].

At USD 4,200,000, this contract far exceeds the [[public procurement threshold]] (source: procurement_contracts_2025.pdf). Investigative priorities:

- Check evaluation panel independence
- Review whether Hansen's involvement was disclosed to the awarding authority
