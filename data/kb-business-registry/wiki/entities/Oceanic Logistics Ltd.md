# Oceanic Logistics Ltd

## Summary

Shipping and logistics company registered in Port City, directed by Maria Gonzalez and Jens Hansen.

## Details

Oceanic Logistics Ltd is registered in the Q1 2026 business registry with registration number BR-2024-00891 (source: business_registry_q1_2026.pdf). Its registered address is 45 Harbor Road, Port City, and its status is active.

The company's directors are Maria Gonzalez and Jens Hansen (listed as "Director Y (alias Jens Hansen)") (source: business_registry_q1_2026.pdf). The use of an alias in the registry is notable and may indicate an attempt to obscure Hansen's involvement. Hansen was later sanctioned in November 2025 for procurement fraud and money laundering (source: sanctions_list_march_2026.pdf).

Oceanic Logistics Ltd was awarded contract PC-2025-00442 (Port Infrastructure Upgrade) for USD 4,200,000, signed by Maria Gonzalez on 2025-08-15 (source: procurement_contracts_2025.pdf). This makes it one of the largest vendors in the 2025 procurement dataset.

The company's co-director relationship with NorthStar Procurement Inc (via Jens Hansen) creates a potential [[conflict of interest]], as both companies operate in overlapping government contracting spaces.
