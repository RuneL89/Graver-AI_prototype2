# Jens Hansen

## Summary

Director of both Oceanic Logistics Ltd and NorthStar Procurement Inc. Sanctioned individual with aliases J. Hansen and Director Y.

## Details

Jens Hansen appears in the Q1 2026 business registry as a director of two separate companies: Oceanic Logistics Ltd (registration BR-2024-00891) and NorthStar Procurement Inc (registration BR-2023-00412) (source: business_registry_q1_2026.pdf). In the registry entry for Oceanic Logistics Ltd, he is listed as "Director Y (alias Jens Hansen)", suggesting a deliberate obfuscation of identity (source: business_registry_q1_2026.pdf).

His dual directorship creates a potential [[conflict of interest]] because NorthStar Procurement Inc is a government vendor while Oceanic Logistics Ltd competes in related logistics markets (source: procurement_contracts_2025.pdf). Hansen is also listed as the signatory director on contract PC-2025-00991 (Government IT Services, USD 1,850,000) on behalf of NorthStar Procurement Inc (source: procurement_contracts_2025.pdf).

The March 2026 sanctions list identifies Hansen as sanctioned on 2025-11-14 for alleged involvement in [[procurement fraud]] and [[money laundering]] (source: sanctions_list_march_2026.pdf). This raises questions about the legitimacy of contracts signed under his authority.
