# Maria Gonzalez

## Summary

Director of Oceanic Logistics Ltd and signatory on the Port Infrastructure Upgrade contract (PC-2025-00442).

## Details

Maria Gonzalez is registered as a director of Oceanic Logistics Ltd (registration BR-2024-00891) alongside Jens Hansen (source: business_registry_q1_2026.pdf). The company is based at 45 Harbor Road, Port City.

In the procurement contracts database, Gonzalez appears as the signatory director on contract PC-2025-00442 — a Port Infrastructure Upgrade awarded to Oceanic Logistics Ltd for USD 4,200,000 on 2025-08-15 (source: procurement_contracts_2025.pdf). This contract is one of the largest infrastructure awards in the 2025 dataset.

Unlike Jens Hansen, Gonzalez does not appear on any sanctions lists. However, her association with Hansen through Oceanic Logistics Ltd may warrant scrutiny given Hansen's later sanctions for procurement fraud (source: sanctions_list_march_2026.pdf).
