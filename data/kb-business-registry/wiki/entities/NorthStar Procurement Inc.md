# NorthStar Procurement Inc

## Summary

Government procurement vendor registered in Capital City, directed by Jens Hansen and Samuel Osei.

## Details

NorthStar Procurement Inc is registered in the Q1 2026 business registry with registration number BR-2023-00412 (source: business_registry_q1_2026.pdf). Its registered address is 88 Commerce Blvd, Capital City. The company has an active status.

The company's directors are Jens Hansen and Samuel Osei (source: business_registry_q1_2026.pdf). Hansen, who is also a director of Oceanic Logistics Ltd, was later sanctioned for alleged procurement fraud and money laundering (source: sanctions_list_march_2026.pdf). This dual directorship and the sanctions against Hansen raise concerns about [[beneficial ownership]] and whether Hansen exercises hidden control over the company's operations.

NorthStar Procurement Inc appears as the vendor on at least one major government contract: PC-2025-00991 (Government IT Services, USD 1,850,000), signed by Jens Hansen on 2025-10-03 (source: procurement_contracts_2025.pdf).
