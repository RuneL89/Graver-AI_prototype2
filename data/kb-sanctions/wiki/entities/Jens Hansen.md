# Jens Hansen

## Summary

Individual sanctioned on 2025-11-14 for alleged involvement in procurement fraud and money laundering. Also known as J. Hansen.

## Details

Jens Hansen appears in the March 2026 sanctions list as an individual sanctioned on 2025-11-14 (source: sanctions_list_march_2026.pdf). The sanctions were imposed for alleged involvement in [[procurement fraud]] and [[money laundering]]. He is also known by the alias J. Hansen and is linked to "offshore shell companies" in addition to his domestic corporate holdings.

### Corporate connections
Hansen is a director of both Oceanic Logistics Ltd and NorthStar Procurement Inc (source: business_registry_q1_2026.pdf). In the registry, he is listed as "Director Y (alias Jens Hansen)" for Oceanic Logistics Ltd — an alias that suggests deliberate identity obfuscation (source: business_registry_q1_2026.pdf).

He also signed contract PC-2025-00991 (Government IT Services, USD 1,850,000) on behalf of NorthStar Procurement Inc on 2025-10-03 (source: procurement_contracts_2025.pdf) — just six weeks before his sanctions were imposed. This timing raises serious questions about whether the awarding authority had any warning of his impending designation.

### Investigative significance
Hansen is the central node connecting all three knowledge bases. His sanctions designation transforms what might appear as routine procurement and registry data into a potential corruption and sanctions-evasion network. The link to "offshore shell companies" (source: sanctions_list_march_2026.pdf) suggests the domestic records may represent only the visible portion of a larger corporate structure.
